Welcome on our website of City Gym

Gym in Brno

About us

City Gym Brno is the biggest gym in the downtown of Brno. Our total area is something about 700 m2 with 60 workout stations for your training. We offer 16 cardio machines from one of the biggest company on the world called Life Fitness and also fitness machines from the Grün Sport which is the best company on the Czech market. For women there is a separated part of the gym only for them. There you can find machines for cardio and fitness which are focused on problematic parts of women body. During your exercise you can enjoy beautiful view to the park.

Where you can find us

City Gym Brno is located between streets Běhounská and Rooseveltová above the Typos passage (club Caribic, Veselá vačice atd.). Entry is located from the street Běhounská 22/24.

How to travel to us

City Gym Brno is located in the downtown of the Brno city. From the traffic hub on the street Česká-Joštova it's something about a minute by foot, as well from Moravské náměstí. From the main train station it's something about 5 minutes by foot as well. If you will come by car, the easiest way where to park is in the parking house of JD (Janáčkovo divadlo) on Moravské náměstí, it's something about 50 meters from the Fitness Center. The parking for our clients has 50% discount!
